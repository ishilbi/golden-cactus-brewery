Golden Cactus Brewing

Our historic, open-air warehouse was originally a soda bottling factory for Royal Crown Cola in the 1950's. Used primarily for industrial storage throughout the latter half of the 20th century, the building was bought by three Virginia Tech college graduates who saw the potential for a great addition to downtown Roanoke's craft-beer scene. Located on the corner of 5th St and Salem Ave, Golden Cactus Brewing opened for business in August of 2020.
